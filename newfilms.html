The Additional Star Wars Films

After the orignal six movies had been released, there was still demand for more movies exploring the Star Wars universe. This sparked another trilogy to be released as well as stand-alone movies.

The sequel trilogy

  1. Star Wars: Episode VII - The Force Awakens
  2. Star Wars: Episode VIII - The Last Jedi
  3. Star Wars: Episode IX - The Rise of Skywalker

The stand-alone movies

  • Star Wars: The Clone Wars
  • Rogue One: A Star Wars Story
  • Solo: A Star Wars Story
