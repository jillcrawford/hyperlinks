The 6 Original Star Wars Films

The original Star Wars movies were released as two trilogies. The first trilogy that was released was the second part of the entire story that was planned to be told.

    The original trilogy

  1. Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope
  2. Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back
  3. Star Wars: Episode VI - The Return of the Jedi

Because the last three movies of this six-movie saga were released first, it sparked additional interest for what the first three movies in the series would have in store. Sixteen years after the original trilogy ended, the first movie in this trilogy was released.

    The prequel trilogy

  1. Star Wars: Episode I - The Phantom Menace
  2. Star Wars: Episode II - Attack of the Clones
  3. Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith
